FAQ

How do telehealth sessions work?

You’ll receive a secure video link before your appointment. Find a quiet, private space and join from a phone, tablet, or computer.

What if I need urgent help?

If you are in crisis, call or text 988 or visit your nearest emergency room. We do not provide emergency services.

Do you offer sliding scale?

Yes—limited sliding‑scale slots are available. Ask during your consult.

Which states do you serve?

Currently: Georgia with Remote services everywhere. We’ll verify your location at intake per licensure rules.
